Paintballers Elite

Competitive Paintball in Hertfordshire

Who are we?

Paintballers Elite are a group that love to play paintball, and would like to add some challenge to the game!


What do we do?

At Paintballers Elite, we meet up for coffee, arrange to play paintball and discuss potential tactics based on game types!

Everything at Paintballers Elite after the meetups, is all paintball! We try to implement the previously spoken about tactics into the games being played!

Where are we based?

Paintballers Elite are mainly based in the St Albans area, which is where most of the meetups will be!


Where do we go?

We go in and around Hertfordshire and the surrounding areas! All paintballing sessions will be in a mixture of places, and our meetups will change from time to time to other locations within Hertfordshire.
